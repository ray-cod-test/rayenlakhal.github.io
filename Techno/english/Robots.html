What is the artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a broad branch of computer science that is concerned with building machines capable of simulating human intelligence that are programmed to think like humans and imitate their actions.

The 4 types of artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence is of four types

  • Reactive machines:
    These AI systems have no memory and have a specific task but since it has no memory, it cannot use past experiences to inform future experiences.
  • Limited memory:
    These AI systems have memory, so they can use past experiences to inform future decisions.
  • Theory of mind:
    This type of AI will be able to infer human intentions and predict behavior, a skill necessary for AI systems to become integral members of the human team.
  • Self-awareness:
    Self-aware machines understand their current state. It is not available now.

Narrow Artificial Intelligence

Narrow AI is all around us and is easily the most successful AI realization to date.

examples:

  • Siri, Alexa
  • Image recognition software
  • Google search
  • IBM's Watson
  • Self-driving cars

The history of Artificial Intelligence

The concept of inanimateness endowed with intelligence has been around since antiquity. Thinkers such as Aristotle used the tools and logic of their time to describe human thought processes as symbols, laying the foundation for artificial intelligence concepts such as the representation of general knowledge.
